RX-78-2

The titular mobile suit of the TV series Mobile Suit Gundam is the RX-78-2 Gundam. It's a member of the RX-78 Gundam series and was secretly created on Side 7. In the One Year War fought by the Earth Federation against the Principality of Zeon, the Gundam would change the course of the conflict in their favor.
